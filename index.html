Grilled Salmon with Avocado Salsa

Dinner

  • 1
  • 15 min
  • Beginner level
  • 248

How to Make Grilled Salmon:

  1. Make the rub and apply it to the salmon. Give it 30 minutes to chill and soak in. Preheat the grill.
  2. Make the salsa: combine the avocado, onion, lemon juice and cilantro. Chill until ready to serve.
  3. Grill the salmon until it flakes with a fork. (about 4 minutes per side.)
  4. Top the cooked salmon with the Avocado Salsa and serve.

Ingredients for Salmon:  salt, chili powder, cumin, paprika, onion powder, black pepper.
Mix these spices together, brush olive oil onto the salmon filets. Cover with the rub mixture, then refrigerate for 30 minutes before grilling.

Ingredients for Salsa:  avocado (cut into cubes), red onion, lime juice, chopped fresh cilantro
Combine everything in a bowl and mix gently. You can keep it chunky or make it a little smoother if you prefer.
